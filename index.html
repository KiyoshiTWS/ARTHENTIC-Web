Digital Protection

What We Are

We want digital artists to feel safe and confident sharing their work online. Our platform is designed to protect your creations, prove ownership, and help you license or sell them securely.

This website provides an all-in-one solution for digital painters, illustrators, and creative studios. You can upload your artworks, register them with proof of authorship, embed protective markers, and monitor online usage to track misuse.

We also support takedown requests for unauthorized uploads and offer licensing tools so you can control how your art is shared. Our goal is simple — you focus on creating, while we focus on protecting.

About Our Mission

We are dedicated to protecting digital artists in today’s fast-moving online world. Our goal is simple: safeguard your creations with proof of authorship, smart protection tools, and legal support when needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

We secure your artwork with a timestamped record of authorship, embed hidden metadata or watermarks, and even provide blockchain-backed certificates. This ensures you can always prove ownership.

We actively monitor online platforms for unauthorized use of your art. If we detect misuse, we assist you with takedown requests and provide evidence of ownership to strengthen your case.

Our platform is designed for freelance artists, illustrators, digital studios, and any creator who shares art online. Whether you’re just starting out or managing a studio, we provide protection at every level.
