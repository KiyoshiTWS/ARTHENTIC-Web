Digital Watermarking

Digital watermarking is a technique used to embed hidden or visible markers in your artwork. These markers help deter theft, prove authorship, and track usage across the internet.

How It Works

  • Invisible watermarks are embedded in the image data and are difficult to remove.
  • Visible watermarks (logos, text) can be placed on top of your artwork for clear protection.
  • Watermarks can include copyright information, timestamps, or unique identifiers.

Why Use Watermarking?

  • Discourage unauthorized use and sharing.
  • Prove ownership in case of disputes.
  • Track your art online and receive alerts for misuse.
